FAQs

How much do your wedding planning services cost?

Our pricing is based on the scope of work and the level of service you need. We offer a variety of different packages, from full-service planning to day-of coordination. We can provide a custom quote after our initial consultation to better understand your specific needs. We work with a variety of budgets, and our goal is to help you allocate your funds effectively. We'll help you prioritize what's most important to you and find the best vendors and options to match your needs.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes, we do! We love working in new locations and are ready to help you plan your special day wherever you choose to celebrate.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend reaching out as soon as possible, ideally 9-12 months in advance for full-service planning and design to give your vision the time and detail it deserves. For partial planning or day-of coordination, we suggest connecting at least 4 months ahead to ensure we can secure your date.
